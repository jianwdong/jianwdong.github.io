About Me

I'm a diagnostic radiologist (nuclear subspecialty) and a data scientist. I have a faculty appointment at the Geisel School of Medicine at Dartmouth, where I practice part-time. Broadly, I am interested in machine learning applications in medicine and healthcare.

Prior to my current roles, I completed my bachelor's in MechE at Columbia, medical school at NYU, a preliminary medicine year at Mount Sinai, radiology residency at BWH/Harvard together with a nuclear medicine fellowship. I am also finishing up my master's in data science at UT Austin. Before medicine, I did dabbled in astrophysics, experimental fluid mechanics, and computational neuroscience. I remain a tinkerer at heart.
